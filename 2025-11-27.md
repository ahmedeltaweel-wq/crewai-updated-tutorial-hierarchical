## 2. EU AI Act negotiations reach final stages; key amendments on foundation models approved

**The Rundown:** Negotiations for the European Union's AI Act are nearing completion, with reports confirming agreement on stricter regulatory requirements for high-impact foundation models and general-purpose AI systems.

**The details:**
*   **Risk-Based Framework:** The Act solidifies a tiered, risk-based approach, applying the most stringent rules to systems deemed "high-risk" (e.g., critical infrastructure, employment, biometric identification, and judicial systems).
*   **Foundation Model Specifics:** Key amendments specifically target General Purpose AI (GPAI) systems, requiring developers of the largest and most powerful models (Foundation Models) to adhere to strict transparency, documentation, and compliance obligations.

*   **Copyright and Data Transparency:** The Act includes mandatory provisions requiring AI model developers to document and disclose detailed summaries of the copyrighted data used for training their models, ensuring better protection for creators and addressing intellectual property concerns.
